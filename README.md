The PheneGene Graphics map for Marfan Syndrome (154700) shows the relationship between the FBN1 gene (134797) and the different diseases it can cause. The map explains that one gene, FBN1, is linked to several related conditions, including Marfan syndrome, Ectopia lentis, MASS syndrome, Weill-Marchesani syndrome, Geleophysic dysplasia, Marfan lipodystrophy, Acromicric dysplasia, and Stiff skin syndrome. This demonstrates that mutations in a single gene can lead to a wide range of different phenotypes.
